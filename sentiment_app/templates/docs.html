How It Works

Paste any text up to 5,000 characters into the analyzer. SentiScope uses a lexicon-based approach — each word is scored against a curated sentiment dictionary and aggregated to produce a final polarity and subjectivity reading.

Scoring Metrics

Polarity: −1.0 → +1.0 Subjectivity: 0.0 → 1.0

Positive (> 0.1)

Text carries an optimistic or favorable emotional tone.

Neutral (−0.1 → 0.1)

Text is factual or emotionally ambiguous. No strong sentiment detected.

Negative (< −0.1)

Text carries a pessimistic or unfavorable emotional tone.

Privacy

SentiScope is stateless. Your text is processed in memory and immediately discarded — nothing is logged, stored, or transmitted to any third-party service.
